How to use:

Select a block type from the palette and click and drag on the squares above to place tiles. When you are done, click "Save". You will receive a string of numbers and commas. Copy this string into a text document on your computer or somewhere else where you can store it. Anytime you want to play your level again, copy your string into the text box and click "Load".
